About XPath Evaluation

XPath (XML Path Language) is a major element in the XSLT standard. It is used to navigate through elements and attributes in an XML document. Our XPath Tester allows you to verify your queries instantly without writing any code.

Privacy & Processing

Just like our other tools, all XPath evaluation happens locally in your browser. Your data is never transmitted to any server.
